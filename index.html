Chole Bhature is a popular North Indian dish featuring spicy chickpea curry (chole) paired with deep-fried bread (bhature). The chickpea curry is cooked with onions, tomatoes, ginger, garlic, and an array of spices for a rich, flavorful taste. Bhature, made from a dough of all-purpose flour and yogurt, is deep-fried until fluffy and golden. Together, they create a mouthwatering combination of textures and flavors, often served with accompaniments like sliced onions, lemon wedges, and pickles. Chole Bhature is cherished for its hearty and indulgent appeal, making it a beloved choice for meals across India and beyond.
